About Us

Leading IT Hardware Supplier in UAE

Colion International Trading LLC is a leading stockist, wholesaler, and exporter of IT hardware, office equipment, and consumer electronics based in the heart of Dubai, UAE. With a solid foundation built on trust, quality, and service excellence, we cater to a diverse global clientele spanning the Middle East, Africa, and other emerging markets.

Since our establishment, we have been committed to delivering genuine, high-quality products sourced directly from the world’s most reputable brands. Our extensive portfolio includes a comprehensive range of laptops, desktops, printers, photocopiers, servers, networking equipment, projectors, surveillance systems, and IT accessories, designed to meet the ever-evolving demands of businesses, resellers, and corporate customers.

At Colion International, we take pride in our:

  • Strong Global Brand Partnerships — authorized reseller and distributor of leading brands such as HP, Lenovo, Epson, Dell, Canon, Brother, Cisco, Hikvision, Microsoft, and more.
  • Large and Ready Stock — substantial inventory levels enable us to offer immediate availability and fast delivery to meet urgent procurement needs.
  • Experienced Team — a highly skilled sales, procurement, and logistics team with deep market knowledge, especially in servicing African, Arab, and international markets.
  • Customer-First Approach — a dedicated commitment to providing personalized service, competitive pricing, and reliable after-sales support.
